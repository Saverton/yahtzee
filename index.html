The Rules of Yahtzee

Objective

Score the maximum possible amount of points by rolling the dice and choosing hands.

Overview

There are 13 different hands, each with their own requirements and scoring procedures. Each hand other than "Yathzee!" can only be scored once, so the player must plan which hands they play each turn to maximize points. Certain hands have specific requirements in order to score, some have specific scoring guidelines, and others have both requirements and scoring guidelines. A list of all the hands and their descriptions can be found below. Bonus points can be earned by meeting a certain point threshold in certain hands and by throwing additional "Yahtzee!"s
